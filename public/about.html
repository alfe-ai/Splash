About Alfe AI

Alfe AI is an open source platform that combines AI-assisted image design, software development, and task management.

All source code is available on GitHub for self-hosting or customization. Alfe AI has been developed with a focus on privacy and security, and your data is only used for purposes required by the application. Data is not sold or used for advertising. When using 3rd party LLM providers, we only use providers that do not train on your data.

Alfe AI is Developed by Lochner Tech.
